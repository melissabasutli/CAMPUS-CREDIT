Loan Options

Campus Credit offers different types of student loans to meet your needs:

  • Tuition Loan: Covers your academic fees and registration costs.


    • Repayment begins 6 months after graduation, with a fixed 5% interest rate.

  • Living Expenses Loan: Helps with accommodation, food, and transport.


    • Flexible repayment plans available, tailored to student budgets.

  • Book & Supplies Loan: Provides funds for textbooks, laptops, and study materials.


    • Quick approval process to ensure you get resources on time.

Choose the loan that best suits your situation and apply online today.

Repayment Plans

Our repayment plans are designed to be flexible and manageable, ensuring you can focus on your studies without the stress of financial burden.

  • Standard Plan: Fixed monthly payments over a 10-year period.
  • Graduated Plan: Lower payments at the start, increasing every 2 years.
  • Income-Driven Plan: Payments based on your income after graduation.
  • Deferred Plan: Payments postponed until after graduation.
