Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for a Campus Credit student loan, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Be a registered student at a recognized college or university.
  • Provide a valid student ID number.
  • Be between the ages of 16 and 25.
  • Provide proof of enrollment (admission letter or transcript).
  • Have a guarantor or parent/guardian for support if required.

Given these requirements, we aim to make the loan application process straightforward and accessible for students who need financial assistance to pursue their education.

Frequently Asked Questions:

  • What documents do I need to provide?

    • To apply, you will need to provide a valid student ID, proof of enrollment (such as an admission letter or transcript), and a guarantor or parent/guardian for support if required.

  • How long does the application process take?

    • Once you submit your application, it typically takes 3-5 business days for us to review and process it. You will be notified of the decision via email.

  • Can I apply for a loan if I am not a full-time student?

    • Yes, you may still be eligible for a Campus Credit student loan if you are enrolled at least half-time in an eligible program.

If you meet these requirements, you can proceed to the application page to apply.

Note: Meeting all eligibility requirements does not guarantee loan approval.
