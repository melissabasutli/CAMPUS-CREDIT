Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for a Campus Credit student loan, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Be a registered student at a recognized college or university.
  • Provide a valid student ID number.
  • Be between the ages of 17 and 25.
  • Provide proof of enrollment (admission letter or transcript).
  • Have a guarantor or parent/guardian for support if required.

If you meet these requirements, you can proceed to the application page to apply.
